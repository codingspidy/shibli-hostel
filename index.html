Facilities at a glance

Bed and Beddings

We provide you comfortable beds & mattresses, bedsheets, pillows, carpets etc. And in winter we also provide you with blankets.

Some extra space

Every room here has a attached bathroom with seperate commode & bathing space, balcony, almirah and a mini kitchen.

Laundary & cleaning

Here we provide you with free laundary and room cleaning services once every week.

AC/ Non AC Rooms

We have rooms with best air conditioning facility and also have Non AC rooms for whom who don't desire AC.

The room options and their respective pricing are as follows -

~ Three tier Non AC - ₹8500

~ Two tier Non AC - ₹9500

~ Three tier AC - ₹10000

~ Two tier AC - ₹11000

Hygienic food & Dining hall

Here you will have a healthy & tasty breakfast, lunch and dinner. And we have a dining hall as well where every student comes and eat.

Drinking water

You will be provided with mineral water for drinking. And there is a water dispenser from where you can get normal, hot & cold water as well for drinking.

Geyser & Refrigeration

There is a geyser for you in every room for taking hot showers and a public refrigerator in which everyone can keep their eatables.

Wifi

In every floor there are 2 unlimited 100Mbps Wifi routers that is made available to you 24*7.

Sports facility

Here we have equipments for different sports like cricket, badminton, table tennis etc present for you.

Management & Security

CCTV cameras are present everywhere except the rooms and everything is under the surveillance of our manager. There is a fingerprint scanner on the main gate inside the hostel through which only registered students & staffs can pass. There are wardens in every floor who look after the students.

Study room

We have a air conditioned study room with a perfect and healthy study atmosphere.

Multipurpose hall

These air conditioned hall have many uses. Family members and friends of students used to meet them here, We offer our Salah in jamaat here, hold quiz nights, movie nights, do study and many more things.

Picnic & outings

Often we go to picnics, have fun and discover new places.
